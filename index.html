About Minecraft Snaps

Minecraft Snaps lets you backup snapshots of your Minecraft worlds so your can revert back to previous versions in case you blow something up.

It stores backups of your worlds in its own app data folder completely separate from the game's data. This way, you can restore worlds even if you run into issues with your Minecraft installation.

Note: Only create new snapshots after saving and closing your map in the game. Also, do not restore snapshots with the map open, or the game will overwrite your restored snapshot when you save.
